  1. To Compress: Paste your plain text (like JSON, CSS, or any text) into the 'Input' box and click 'Compress'.
  2. The tool will Gzip your text and show a Base64 string in the 'Output' box. This string is your compressed text.
  3. To Decompress: Paste a Base64 Gzip string (like one you just made) into the 'Input' box and click 'Decompress'.
  4. The original, readable text will appear in the 'Output' box.
  5. Compression stats will show you how much space you saved!

Gzip Compressor & Decompressor

Compress plain text into a Gzip Base64 string, or decompress it back.
